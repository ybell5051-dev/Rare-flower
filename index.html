A New Project By Xtheflare

Rare Flowers

Bloom where they tried to break you.

One Story. Two Worlds.

There's a version of every song that starts before it's a song. Before the studio, before the mix — it's just a voice and a guitar and whatever I was going through. That's where Rare Flowers starts.

These songs came from a stretch where I was losing almost everything. What I never lost was the belief I had in myself. Like a flower breaking through concrete, each track is a piece of that growth. Soul Rock at its core: raw and unfiltered.

Right alongside it, there's The Frequency.

The AI-driven universe where Zeal Von Rebel and DJ Zuri Wave live. It's the other side of the coin—a full project of 10+ tracks of music, visuals, and an expanding cyberpunk narrative.
